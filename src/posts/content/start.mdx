I’ve always loved how music connects people — the songs we love say so much about who we are. But most apps today focus on *listening*, not *expressing*. I wanted to change that.

**Wheelhouse** is my attempt to bring back that personal, creative energy we used to feel making mixtapes or burning CDs for friends. It’s like your own music journal — but social, fun, and designed to show off your taste.

You can:

- Track the songs and albums you love  
- Make creative playlists that reflect your vibe  
- Share your music taste with friends in a more expressive way  

Think of it like **Letterboxd**, but for music. It’s not just about what you listen to — it’s about what those songs *mean* to you. Whether it's your current obsessions, all-time favorites, or deep cuts only real ones know — Wheelhouse gives you a space to share that side of yourself.

I’ve got a lot of ideas for where to take this project next, and I’ll be sharing more soon. For now, I just wanted to give a glimpse of what I’m building.

If you love music and want to express your taste beyond playlists, I hope you’ll stick around.
